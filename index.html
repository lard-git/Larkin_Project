Who We Are

Our Story

Founded originally as an IT firm specializing in products with technological advancements in 2013, the company has made steady inroad into the growing market of Papua New Guinea through reliable product delivery and aftersales services.

With the company's extensive knowledge in import-export systems and direct connections with overseas manufacturers and large-scale suppliers, Larkin Enterprises competes with leading market players in PNG — promoting Greater Opportunity in the workplace, the marketplace and the community.

As Papua New Guinea's economy and popularity grows across the Pacific, the company expanded into high quality, state-of-the-art products including IT equipment, air-conditioning systems, tracking and security devices, noise reduction products and building materials.
